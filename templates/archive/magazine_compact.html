A Comprehensive Roadmap to Transform the Platform

The Solon Tax platform stands at an inflection point. While the foundation is strong, critical modernization investments and core revenue capabilities are essential to compete in 2026 and capture the €200M+ project pipeline ahead.

This roadmap combines two strategic initiatives: platform modernization (hexagonal architecture, Java 25, Spring Boot 4.x, Oracle 26ai) and core revenue management (registration, accounting, billing, penalties, tax accounts). Together, they position Solon Tax as a comprehensive, scalable solution supporting Nordic and European market expansion.
