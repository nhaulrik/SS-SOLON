Key Investment Benefits
01
Faster Taxpayer Onboarding Reduces manual registration effort and onboarding time — enabling tax authorities to scale intake without proportional resource growth.
02
Foundational for All Revenue Flows Every downstream capability — billing, assessments, accounting — depends on accurate registration data. This initiative de-risks the entire roadmap.
03
Multi-Jurisdiction Ready Org hierarchy and backdate support enables complex tax structures across jurisdictions, directly supporting GRC and future client implementations.
Capability Themes — What this initiative delivers
🪪
Party Identity & Identifiers
Establishes how taxpayers and organisations are uniquely identified across the system — supporting multiple identifier types, deduplication, and national registry alignment.
🏢
Organisation Hierarchy & History
Enables multi-level party role structures and full backdate change history — critical for complex tax jurisdictions managing corporate groups and role transitions.
🔍
Search & Data Governance
Enhances registry search with governance-controlled result limits, relevant filters, and improved UX — ensuring officers can locate the right party record efficiently.
🌐
Self-Service & Portal Registration
Allows taxpayers to self-register and manage their profiles via the taxpayer portal, reducing administrative burden and enabling 3rd-party delegate access.
🔗
API & Integration Layer
Exposes registration capabilities via external API endpoints and a notification engine — enabling downstream systems and taxpayer confirmations to be triggered automatically.
📊
Reporting & Audit
Provides full audit trail of registration events and KPI dashboards for officer workload — supporting compliance and operational oversight for tax administrators.
