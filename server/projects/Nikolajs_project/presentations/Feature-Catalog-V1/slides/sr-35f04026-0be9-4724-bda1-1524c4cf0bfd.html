Why It's Important
01
Improved User Experience
Allows users to upload and manage attachments during the form-filling process (pre-save), providing form filing steps clarity and reducing unnecessary interruptions.
02
Fewer Incomplete Submissions
Reduces delays caused by missing attachments and ensures higher data integrity by allowing documents to be linked directly to relevant parties/roles at the point of entry.
03
Enhanced Form Management
Enables users to view, add, remove, and edit attachment metadata both before and after saving, with full audit trail logging of all modifications.
Key Epics & Features
Upload Before Save
Users (revenue officer or taxpayer) can upload attachments with permitted metadata while filling the form, before saving, including during registration forms where the relevant party is not yet created.
View and Manage Attachments
Users can view the list of uploaded attachments while filling the form (before saving) and manage attachments before and after saving, including adding new attachments, removing attachments, and editing metadata.
Link Attachments to Records
In RM, users can link attachments to additional system records (e.g., case file) while managing form attachments, enabling comprehensive document management across related records.
Failure Handling
If attachment upload fails, the user is clearly informed by a message that attachment was not successfully uploaded. Form saving/submission is not blocked and successfully uploaded files remain attached.
Audit Trail Logging
All attachment modifications after saving are stored in audit log, providing complete traceability and compliance with regulatory requirements for document management.
Non-Functional Requirements
Team alignment on Performance, Security, Accessibility, and Usability standards with comprehensive testing to ensure DODs are fulfilled and no issues are created.
