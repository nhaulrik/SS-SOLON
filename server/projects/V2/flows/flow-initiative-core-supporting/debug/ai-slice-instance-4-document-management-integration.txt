=== Strategic Case — Why Prioritise This Initiative ===

Reason 01 Title: Enable Digital Signature Validation
Reason 01 Text: Capability to validate digital signatures on attachments ensures document authenticity and compliance with electronic signature regulations, protecting revenue authority from fraudulent submissions.

Reason 02 Title: Support System-Generated Document Signing
Reason 02 Text: Automatic digital signing of system-generated documents by BPMN processes enables secure distribution of official documents with embedded signatures, supporting regulatory compliance and audit requirements.

Reason 03 Title: Enhance Document Security Framework
Reason 03 Text: Comprehensive document management integration with signature validation and system signing capabilities provides foundation for secure document exchange with taxpayers and external stakeholders.
