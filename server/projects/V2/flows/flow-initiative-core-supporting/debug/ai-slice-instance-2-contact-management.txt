=== Strategic Case — Why Prioritise This Initiative ===

Reason 01 Title: Ensure Legal Document Compliance
Reason 01 Text: Automatic assignment of unique registration numbers to incoming and outgoing documents ensures compliance with legal requirements for document identification, enabling audit trails and regulatory reporting across all document types.

Reason 02 Title: Enable Multi-Recipient Communication
Reason 02 Text: Capability to send outbound messages to multiple recipients simultaneously with template-based content variation ensures consistent communication with taxpayers, representatives, and agents while reducing manual effort.

Reason 03 Title: Support Flexible Contact Distribution
Reason 03 Text: Admin-configurable rules for recipient selection and message generation enable organisations to define complex communication workflows, supporting diverse business scenarios without code changes.
