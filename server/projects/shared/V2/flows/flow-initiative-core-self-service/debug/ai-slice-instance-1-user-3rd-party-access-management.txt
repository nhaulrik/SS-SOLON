=== Strategic Case — Why Prioritise This Initiative ===

Reason 01 Title: Implement Multi-Level Representation Framework
Reason 01 Text: Support two-way, multi-level representations with configurable lifecycle and datetime validity, enabling complex delegation hierarchies required by tax authorities.

Reason 02 Title: Enforce Temporal Access Control Precision
Reason 02 Text: Capture start and end dates with timezone support for representations and user rights, ensuring accurate permission enforcement across date boundaries.

Reason 03 Title: Enable RM-Based Representation Management
Reason 03 Text: Revenue management users manage taxpayer authorizations directly, synchronizing with self-service in real-time and preventing conflicting delegations.
