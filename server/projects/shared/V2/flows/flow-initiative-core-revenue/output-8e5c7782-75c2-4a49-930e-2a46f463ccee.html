01
Correct Interest Calculation for Unallocated Payments
Current system calculates credit interest only on bill segment balances, ignoring unallocated payment segments. When liability amendments occur, resulting credits earn no interest. Enhancement ensures taxpayers receive proper reimbursement interest on all credit amounts, improving fairness and regulatory compliance.
02
Support Tax Account Implementation Requirements
Northern country implementations require accurate interest calculation across all payment segments for tax account scenarios. System must consider both allocated and unallocated payments to ensure final interest calculations are accurate and usable in relevant processes.
03
Address Multiple Unallocated Payment Scenarios
Unallocated payment segments arise from payment suspense, processing errors, insufficient liability balances, and payment event state transitions. Enhancement handles all these business cases, preventing unfair situations where taxpayer funds held by authority do not accrue required interest.
Key Features
Enhance Credit Interest Calculation to include unallocated payment segments
Update CREDIT_INT penalty and interest rule to calculate reimbursement interest on credit unallocated payment segments alongside bill segment credits, ensuring accurate interest for tax account scenarios.
