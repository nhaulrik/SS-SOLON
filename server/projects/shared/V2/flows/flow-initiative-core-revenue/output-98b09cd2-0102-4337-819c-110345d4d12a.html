01
Accurate Interest Calculation on Unallocated Payments
System currently calculates interest only on bill segment balances, excluding unallocated payment segments. This creates unfair situations where taxpayer funds held by tax authority do not accrue required interest. Enhancement ensures proper interest calculation for all credit amounts.
02
Support Tax Account Implementation
Northern countries implementing Tax Account scenarios require comprehensive interest calculation including both allocated and unallocated payment segments. This is essential for accurate financial transaction processing and taxpayer fairness in credit interest scenarios.
03
Regulatory Compliance for Interest Accrual
Multiple business cases generate unallocated payment segments: payment suspense, processing errors, insufficient liability balance, and payment event state transitions. System must calculate interest on all credit amounts to comply with tax authority obligations and fairness principles.
Key Features
Enhance Credit Interest Calculation to include unallocated payment segments
Update CREDIT_INT penalty and interest rule to calculate reimbursement interest on credit unallocated payment segments alongside bill segment credits for accurate taxpayer interest accrual.
