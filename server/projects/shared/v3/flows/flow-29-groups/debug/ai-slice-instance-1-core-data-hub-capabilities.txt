{color:#505f79}1. Invoice Creation (Seller ERP/Accounting System){color}
{color:#505f79}Seller generates invoice in EN 16931 compliant format (structured XML/UBL).{color}
{color:#505f79}Integration Needed: ERP/accounting system must be able to export EN 16931 e-invoic{color}es.

_2._ *Submission / Pre-Validation (National Digital Reporting System, DRR)*
Invoice data is sent electronically to the tax authority before being delivered to the buyer.
Integration Options:  Direct API from ERP to tax authority system, or  PEPPOL Access Point that connects ERP  TO  tax authority network endpoint.
Purpose: pre-validation and acknowledgment.

*3. Validation by Tax Authority*
Tax authority checks: Invoice format (EN 16931 compliance) ,  VAT numbers and amounts ,  Business rules and optional risk checks
Outcome process: 
Accepted: unique ID and timestamp assigned
Rejected : correction requested
Integration Needed: Validation engine integrated with DRR database & ERP feedback mechanism.

{color:#505f79}4.   Invoice Delivery to Buyer
Once validated, invoice is transmitted electronically to the buyer system (ERP or e-invoicing platform).{color}
{color:#505f79}Integration Needed: PEPPOL or other secure delivery channels to buyer system.{color}

*5. Storage and EU Data Exchange*
Tax authority stores invoice data in national database.
For cross-border transactions, summary data or full invoices may be shared with EU Member States via VIES 2.0.
Integrations Needed:   
National DB from and to  VIES 2.0 interface 
Internal reporting / archival systems
Long-term *archive* of e-invoices, {*}logs{*}, and *declarations* / Security (data retention)
GDPR

*6. Reconciliation / VAT Return & Compliance*
Invoice data is used to:
Auto-populate VAT returns for the taxpayer
Pre-fill VAT declarations in OSS/IOSS or national system /Extend OSS usage   (?) 
Share summary data with EU authorities for cross-border reconciliation
Integration Needed:
DRR from/to VAT return processing system,
OSS/IOSS connectors
(Low- Optional) Payment & Banking Interfaces to verify VAT collection

*7. Monitoring and Risk Analysis*
Real-time analytics for:  Cross-checking declarations , Fraud detection ,  Audit trails and compliance reporting
Integration Needed: DRR / national DB ↔ Analytics engines / Risk management platforms

 

Other: 
Make sure the exceptions of any failures are logged
