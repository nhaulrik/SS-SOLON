Issue Type: Feature | IRM: IRM-30418 | Roadmap Initiative Group: CORE SUPPORTING CAPABILITIES | Roadmap Initiative: UI CONFIGURATION | Roadmap Feature: Configurable Home Page widgets  in RM | Requester: GRC | PI: PI29 | Estimate: 224 | Feature Priority: p3 | Feature Status: To Do | Features Description: *RM Home page  {color:#c1c7d0}_- not discussed with customers:_{color}* 
Home page may be customizable by user (or per groups) . Several widgets may be available and can be docked in the page as per user preferences. 
The home page acts as a central command center, letting a tax officer or user quickly see tasks, deadlines, notifications, and insights without navigating through multiple screens.

_Examples of widgets:_ 
 * _My Calendar, My team calendar, My Pending tasks, Overdue tasks,_ _Unassigned tasks, <status> tasks_
 * _Summary of pending approvals, or submissions._
 * _Quick Search in Registry  (TBC due to complexity)_ 
 * _Quick search taxpayers_
 * _Create shortcuts / Quick links to usually used tabs/records/other links  (other apps, to taxpayers, cases, documents, easily access payplans, or form list page with predefine filter etc)_ 
 * _Notifications / Alerts /   (deadlines, system messages, process completions/errors... )_

 

Complex in BE or not ready in BE or by examples 
 * _My Recent Items (Accessed records)_ 
 * _Announcements from the company (TBD) or Integration with other internal used messaging systems_

 * _Other  Dashboards and KPIs /reports and quick metrics  (e.g. Team allocation for managers,  returned payouts , collection rates, or workload by officer , new registration forms, total taxpayers managed, open cases, or recent filings.  )_ 

Prerequisites :
I can see my calendar or my team calendar, quick search available , recent records accessed
