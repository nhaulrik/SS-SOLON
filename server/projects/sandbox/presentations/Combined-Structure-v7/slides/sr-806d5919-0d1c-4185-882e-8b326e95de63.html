Strategic Rationale Why It Matters
01
Empower Self-Service Access Management
Enables taxpayers and representatives to independently manage authorizations and delegations with full auditability — reducing administrative burden on tax authority staff while maintaining strict governance and compliance controls.
02
Multi-Level Representation & 2-Way Authorization
Supports complex delegation chains and bidirectional authorization flows — enabling sophisticated business scenarios such as legal representatives delegating to accounting firms, with full lifecycle management and automatic expiration enforcement.
03
Enhanced Form Management & Document Handling
Allows pre-save attachment uploads and form-type-based service definitions — improving user experience, reducing incomplete submissions, and enabling seamless integration with external systems via APIs and form types.
Business Value & Market Relevance
Business Value
  • Reduces manual authorization management workload — freeing tax authority staff for higher-value compliance activities
  • Enables complex multi-level delegation scenarios with automatic enforcement and audit trails — improving governance and fraud prevention
  • Pre-save document uploads and form-type-based services improve taxpayer experience and reduce submission errors
  • Real-time RM/SS synchronization and automated expiration enforcement eliminate stale permissions and unauthorized access risks
Market & Strategic Relevance
  • Positions platform as a modern, user-centric tax solution — critical differentiator in competitive procurement
  • Supports integration with external ERP systems and third-party service providers via API-based access controls
  • Demonstrates strong governance and compliance posture — essential for regulated tax authority deployments
  • Enables self-service capabilities that reduce total cost of ownership and improve citizen/business satisfaction
