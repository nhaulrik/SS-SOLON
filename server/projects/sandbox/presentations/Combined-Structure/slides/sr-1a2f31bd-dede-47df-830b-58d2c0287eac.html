PI 28
Strategic Rationale Why It Matters
01
Enhanced Revenue Management Capabilities
Expands core revenue management functionality across billing, assessments, tax accounts, and penalty & interest calculations — enabling revenue authorities to manage complex tax scenarios with greater accuracy and operational efficiency.
02
Improved Taxpayer Accounting & GL Integration
Delivers advanced taxpayer accounting features including GL decision tables with multiline rules, extensibility for high-volume decisions, and enhanced financial transaction logging — supporting complex accounting requirements across organizational units and claimants.
03
Registry & Data Management Excellence
Introduces enhanced validity period conditions, organizational hierarchy visualization, and comprehensive search capabilities — enabling revenue officers to manage party relationships, tax accounts, and financial data with greater precision and visibility.
Business Value & Market Relevance
Business Value
  • Enables accurate credit interest calculation including unallocated payment segments — ensuring fair taxpayer treatment and compliance with interest regulations
  • Supports multiple payment installment actions for ad-hoc obligations — expanding billing flexibility for inheritance tax, legacy migrations, and guarantee scenarios
  • Introduces configurable GL decision tables with multiline rules — reducing configuration complexity and supporting hundreds of special accounting cases
  • Provides tax account overview with period-centric financial transaction views — improving revenue officer efficiency and accuracy in tax account management
Market & Strategic Relevance
  • Addresses Nordic and Scandinavian tax authority requirements for tax account management and reconciliation — critical for Northern European market expansion
  • Supports organizational unit and claimant financial event logging — enabling complex inter-departmental and third-party settlement scenarios required by modern revenue authorities
  • Enhances party hierarchy visualization and validity period management — improving operational transparency and compliance with complex registry requirements
  • Positions Solon as a comprehensive, extensible revenue management platform capable of handling diverse international tax scenarios and regulatory frameworks
