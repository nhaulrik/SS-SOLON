Strategic Rationale Why It Matters
01
Enhanced User Interface Configuration
Delivers UI/UX improvements including field prefilling, text capitalization, visual indicators for required fields, and enhanced form field configurability — improving user experience and reducing data entry errors across all revenue management modules.
02
Robust Form Processing & Metadata Management
Enables comprehensive form submission tracking with creator/submitter logging, user-defined characteristics, and notes functionality — supporting auditability, traceability, and efficient data migration across systems.
03
EU Tax Compliance & Document Management
Implements historical code tracking for NACE/VAT compliance, system digital signing of documents, signature validation, and unique document numbering — ensuring legal compliance with EU regulations and secure document handling.
Business Value & Market Relevance
Business Value
  • Improves user experience through intelligent field prefilling and visual cues — reducing data entry time and errors
  • Enables comprehensive audit trails for form submissions — supporting regulatory compliance and operational transparency
  • Supports EU tax code evolution (NACE Rev. 2.1) with historical tracking — ensuring accurate backdated processing and reporting
  • Automates document signing and validation — eliminating manual steps and strengthening document authenticity
Market & Strategic Relevance
  • Demonstrates compliance with EU tax regulations and digital signature standards (PAdES) — critical for European revenue authority deployments
  • Strengthens platform configurability and flexibility — enabling rapid customization for diverse project requirements
  • Enhances document governance and legal validity — supporting secure distribution of officially signed documents to taxpayers
  • Reduces operational effort through automation and intelligent UI — improving total cost of ownership and user satisfaction
